DESERT FIREWORKS

A quiet desert. A crate that never runs out. You, a torch, and the night sky.

In VR (Quest)

  • Grip — grab a firework or the torch
  • Push the stick into the sand and let go to plant it — any angle you like
  • Touch the torch flame to a fuse to light it… then step back
  • Left stick — walk  ·  Right stick — snap turn

On Desktop

  • WASD + mouse — move and look (click to capture the mouse)
  • E — grab / drop  ·  Scroll — tilt held firework
  • Left click — plant held firework / light a fuse with the torch
  • F — quick-launch a random rocket (lazy mode)

Sound on. Everything you hear is synthesized live — turn it up for the bass.
